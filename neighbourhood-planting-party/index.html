Neighbourhood Planting Party

A Neighbourhood Planting Party has become a very poplar idea. By neighbours getting together to work on their properties, the work becomes fun. Turn on the music, bring out the refreshments and have a fun filled day while watching your neighbourhood increase in value.

Landscaping as been shown to be the best investment you can make with your home. The second best is when your neighbourhood has been landscaped. A common theme that ties a neighbourhood really makes a difference.

This idea allows strong neighbours to help less able ones and for experienced gardeners to share their knowledge with novices.

To help this great idea, we offer a special delivery arrangements. We only charge the flat rate for one drop-off regardless of how many drops are needed in your neighbourhood. All members of the group need to be located within 1/2 kilometer of each other.   


Don’t delay, talk to your neighbour today and get things rolling!
